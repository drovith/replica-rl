The menu · Late autumn

What the valley is giving this week.

Our menu is printed fresh each Wednesday, so this is a snapshot rather than a promise — dishes come and go with the harvest. Below is what we're cooking right now, the week of the first frost.

How to eat here

Build your own meal, or leave it to us

Most tables share two or three smaller plates to begin, then take a larger plate each. If you'd rather not decide, ask for the Kitchen's Choice — a five-course tasting of whatever Esme is most excited about that evening, at £58 a head.

Please tell us about allergies and dietaries when you book. Almost everything can be adapted, and our team knows every ingredient on the plate by name and field.

A slower kind of Sunday

The harvest lunch, served family-style

Every Sunday from noon we lay out a single feast for the whole room — a roast of the week, a table of seasonal sides, and a help-yourself pudding. Three courses, brought to the middle of the table, eaten the long way round.

£34 per person · children £16 · vegetarian feast always available. Booking strongly recommended.
