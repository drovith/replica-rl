Book in under a minute

Tables can be reserved up to eight weeks ahead. For parties of seven or more, or to book the whole room, please call us directly so we can look after you properly.

We'll confirm your booking by email or text within the hour. No deposit needed for tables of six or fewer.

Finding the mill

Down Mill Lane, where the river bends

Follow the high street through Harlow Creek village and turn down Mill Lane beside the stone bridge. The restaurant is at the very end, where the lane meets the water — you'll see the old waterwheel and the lit windows before you see the sign.

  • Free parking in the mill yard, with two accessible bays
  • A flat riverside footpath leads from the village square, five minutes
  • Marsden station is nine minutes by taxi; we're happy to call one for you
  • Secure bike racks under cover by the terrace

Before you come

A few house notes

Can you cater for dietary needs?

Yes — vegetarian and vegan menus run every service, and we adapt for allergies and intolerances daily. Tell us when you book and our kitchen, which knows every ingredient by name, will take care of the rest.

Are children welcome?

Very. We love a family table, especially at Sunday lunch, and offer smaller portions of the day's menu for younger diners. High chairs are available — just let us know.

Do you take walk-ins?

We hold a handful of tables and bar seats back each evening for people passing through. Weekends are tight, so a quick call ahead is always worth it.

Is there a dress code?

None at all. Come as you are — we've welcomed muddy walking boots and wedding suits at neighbouring tables on the same night.

Can we buy a gift voucher?

We sell vouchers for any value and for the Kitchen's Choice tasting. Email hello@sorrelandstone.co and we'll post a hand-written one out, or have it ready to collect.

We'll keep the candles lit.
