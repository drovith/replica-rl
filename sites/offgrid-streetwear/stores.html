Before you ask

FAQ

Will you restock something I missed?
No. It’s the one promise we never break. When a piece sells out it’s retired to the archive permanently — no reprints, no “back in stock,” no quiet second pull. The drop list is the only way to be early next time.
How does sizing run?
Most pieces are cut boxy and oversized — take your true size for a relaxed fit, or size down for something structured. Every product page lists flat measurements and the model’s height and size so you can compare against something you own.
Where do you ship and how much?
We ship worldwide from Manchester. UK orders are free over £120 (otherwise £4.50) and arrive in 2–3 days. EU and rest-of-world are calculated at checkout; duties for non-UK orders are the buyer’s responsibility. Everything goes out within 48 hours.
What’s your returns policy?
14 days for unworn pieces with tags attached, for a refund or size swap while stock lasts. Because runs are tiny, a swap isn’t always possible — if your size has sold out we’ll refund instead. Sale and archive items are final.
Can my shop stock REFUSE?
Possibly. We add one or two stockists a year and we’re fussy about fit — independent, point-of-view shops only, no department-store concessions. Email press@refuse-goods.xyz with your shop, your city and why it makes sense.
