Billing questions

The fine print, in plain English.

Q. Is Novault really free to use?

Yes. Downloading, creating wallets, holding, sending and receiving are all free. You only pay network gas (set by the chain) and a small fee on optional swaps, bridges and on-ramps.

Q. What does Novault Plus actually save me?

Plus halves the in-app swap and bridge fee to 0.25%, adds MEV protection and tax exports. If you swap more than about $1,600 a month, it usually pays for itself.

Q. Why is there a swap fee at all?

It funds development, audits and the $2M bug bounty — instead of selling your data or padding exchange rates. It's flat and shown before you confirm.

Q. Can I pay for Plus with crypto?

Yes. Subscribe with USDC, ETH or SOL directly from your wallet, or use card and Apple Pay. No bank required to go Plus.

Q. Do you take a cut of staking rewards?

No. Novault charges $0 to stake. You only pay the validator's standard commission, which we display before you delegate.

Q. What about Teams pricing?

Teams is priced per seat based on size and features like multisig and audit logs. Reach out and we'll put together a quote within a day.
