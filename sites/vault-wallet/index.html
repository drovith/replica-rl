Why Novault

A wallet that respects the word ownership.

Most "wallets" are just a login. Novault is built around a single principle: only you can touch your funds. Everything else — the speed, the design, the chains — is in service of that.

True self-custody

Your recovery phrase is generated on-device and encrypted with secure-enclave hardware. We literally cannot access, freeze or move your assets.

One wallet, 40+ chains

Ethereum, Solana, Bitcoin, Base, Arbitrum, Polygon and more — all under a single recovery phrase, with a unified portfolio and history.

Swaps in seconds

Built-in aggregator routes every trade across DEXs and bridges to find the best price, with the network fee shown up front. No hidden spread.

Transaction previews

Before you sign, Novault simulates the result so you see exactly what leaves your wallet — and flags malicious approvals before they cost you.

Mobile & extension

The same vault on iOS, Android and your browser. Approve a dapp on desktop, confirm on your phone — your keys stay where you put them.

Cloud-free by design

No accounts, no email, no KYC to hold crypto. Your portfolio isn't tied to a server that can go down, get hacked, or sell your data.

Self-custody, made human

Security you don't need a PhD to use.

Self-custody used to mean scribbling twelve words on paper and praying. Novault keeps that power but removes the panic — with on-device backups, guided recovery, and clear warnings the moment something looks wrong.

  • Encrypted, optional iCloud / Google Drive recovery — sealed so even we can't read it
  • Biometric unlock and per-transaction Face ID confirmation
  • Real-time scam, phishing and malicious-contract detection
  • Optional hardware wallet pairing for cold storage
