Practice 01

Mergers & Acquisitions

The deal is rarely won in the documents. It is won in the structure, the sequencing, and the judgment to know which points to hold and which to trade.

We represent founders, boards, and sponsors across the transaction lifecycle — from the first confidential approach through closing and the integration that follows. Our partners have papered hundreds of combinations and bring that pattern recognition to every term sheet.

Sell-Side Buy-Side Carve-Outs Recapitalizations Earnouts Joint Ventures

Representative Engagements

  • $480M sale of a Series C climate-tech company to a strategic acquirer.
  • Sponsor-backed roll-up of four healthcare services businesses with simultaneous closings.
  • Carve-out divestiture of a hardware division, including a transition services agreement.
  • Management buyout of a family-owned manufacturer with rollover equity.

Practice 02

Intellectual Property

For many of our clients, the IP is the company. We treat it that way — as the asset your valuation, your moat, and your next deal depend on.

We build portfolio strategy, negotiate licensing and technology agreements, run IP diligence on both sides of a transaction, and resolve disputes before they become litigation. When a deal turns on who owns what, we are the lawyers in the room who can answer the question.

Portfolio Strategy Licensing Tech Transactions IP Diligence Trade Secrets Disputes

Representative Engagements

  • Cross-border patent licensing program for a semiconductor designer.
  • IP carve-out and clean-room diligence ahead of a strategic acquisition.
  • Open-source compliance review for a developer-tools company pre-Series B.
  • Pre-litigation resolution of a trade-secret dispute between former co-founders.

Practice 03

Venture Financing

We have sat on both sides of the term sheet — for the founder raising and the fund investing — which means we know exactly where the real leverage lives.

From the first convertible note through growth equity and beyond, we counsel companies on raising capital and investors on deploying it. We also form the funds themselves. Our goal is a financing that closes cleanly today and does not create problems for the round, or the exit, that comes next.

Seed & SAFEs Priced Rounds Growth Equity Fund Formation Secondaries Cap Tables

Representative Engagements

  • $120M Series B for an enterprise AI platform with an option-pool reset.
  • Formation of a $90M early-stage venture fund and its first three investments.
  • Founder-side counsel on a competitive Series A with structured liquidation terms.
  • Tender-offer secondary providing early liquidity to employees and seed investors.
