Our services

Everything little teeth need,
nothing they don't.

We focus only on children, so every treatment is sized, paced, and explained for a young patient. Here's the gentle, plain-language version of what we do.

Book a first visit

Every six months

Checkups & cleanings

The heart of preventive care. We gently count and examine each tooth, polish away plaque, check how the jaw and bite are growing, and finish with a smooth fluoride coat. For first-timers, we move at the speed of the child — even if that means just a ride in the chair this time.

  • Gentle exam
  • Soft polish
  • Fluoride varnish
  • Growth & bite check
  • Brushing coaching
Quick & painless

Sealants & fluoride

The chewing surfaces of back teeth are full of tiny grooves where food and bacteria hide — and where most childhood cavities begin. A dental sealant is a thin, protective coat we paint onto those grooves. There's no drilling and no numbing; it takes just a few minutes per tooth and can prevent cavities for years.

  • No drilling
  • No needles
  • 5 minutes per tooth
  • Great for new molars
When teeth need a little help

Gentle fillings & treatment

If a cavity does turn up, we treat it calmly and tooth-colored, so it blends right in. We use the smallest effective numbing, walk children through every sound and sensation beforehand, and offer "laughing gas" (nitrous oxide) for kids who'd feel more relaxed with it. Most fillings are done before a child even realises we've started.

  • Tooth-coloured fillings
  • Gentle numbing
  • Nitrous oxide available
  • Step-by-step explanations
The right care at the right time

Orthodontic referrals

We don't place braces ourselves — and that's a good thing. It means our advice is unbiased. We keep a close eye on how your child's bite, spacing, and jaw are developing, and when the timing is right we refer you to one of our trusted local orthodontists, with a full handover of records so you never start from scratch.

  • Bite & spacing monitoring
  • Trusted local specialists
  • Records shared for you
  • No-pressure guidance
Care that adapts

Special & sensory-friendly care

Every child deserves a dentist who meets them where they are. We offer quiet, low-stimulation rooms, noise-cancelling headphones, weighted lap blankets, social stories you can read at home before the visit, and longer appointments with the same familiar faces. Tell us what helps your child feel safe, and we'll build the visit around it.

  • Quiet rooms
  • Pre-visit social stories
  • Familiar team each time
  • Desensitisation visits
When you need us fast

Same-day emergencies

Knocked-out tooth at the park? Toothache that started at midnight? We hold space in every day's schedule for urgent visits and offer an after-hours advice line so you're never left guessing. Call us first — we'll tell you exactly what to do in the moment and get you seen quickly.

  • Same-day slots
  • After-hours advice line

  • Pain relief guidance

Common questions

Good things parents ask us.

When should my child first see a dentist?

By their first birthday, or within six months of the first tooth appearing — whichever comes first. Early visits are short and friendly, and they help us catch tiny issues before they grow while building a happy relationship with the dentist.

Are baby teeth really worth treating?

Absolutely. Baby teeth hold space for adult teeth, help with speech and eating, and can cause real pain or infection if a cavity is left. Caring for them well sets up healthier permanent teeth later.

Do you take X-rays of young children?

Only when we genuinely need the information, and always with the lowest safe dose and protective coverings. For many young children, a visual exam is plenty. We'll always explain why before recommending one.

What if my child is too nervous to cooperate?

That's completely normal and we plan for it. We use gentle, gradual "desensitisation" visits where the only goal is to feel comfortable. There's no pressure and no judgement — just patience and small wins.

Not sure what your child needs?

Start with a checkup. We'll take a look, talk it through in plain language, and only ever recommend what's truly needed.
