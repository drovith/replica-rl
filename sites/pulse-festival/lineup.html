LINEUP

Eighty-plus acts. Six stages. One ridiculous weekend. From stadium-ready headliners to the band you'll discover at 3am and never shut up about. This is the full bill — phase one and two combined, with a handful of late additions still to come.

Aurora Static

Electro-pop maximalists with a six-piece live band and a light rig that could be seen from space. Closing Friday night with the sea at their backs.

Velvet Mariner

Cinematic soul and brass for miles. Last year's word-of-mouth sensation, back to close the biggest night of the weekend.

Soul Funk

Sunday · The High Tide

The Lumens

Anthemic indie rock built for singing into the dark. The perfect, slightly tearful, send-off to three days on the sand.
