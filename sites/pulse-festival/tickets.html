Ticket questions

Good to know

How do the price tiers work?

Tickets are released in tiers. When a tier sells out, the price rises to the next one — it never comes back down. Tier 1 is the cheapest you'll ever see it, so early birds win.

Can I get a refund?

Tickets are refundable up to 14 days after purchase, or fully transferable to a friend any time before the festival via your account. We also offer free ticket protection at checkout.

Do under-18s need a ticket?

Under-18s must be accompanied by a ticket-holding adult (one adult per two minors). Under-12s go free but still need a free child wristband, booked in advance. The Undertow is strictly 18+ after 23:00.

When will I get my wristband?

E-tickets land in your inbox instantly. Physical wristbands are exchanged at the box office on arrival — bring your QR code and matching ID. Camping wristbands open Thursday evening.

Is the festival cashless?

Yes. All bars, traders and stalls are card and contactless only. There are free top-up and ATM points by the main gate if you'd rather load a card.
