Our story

We're a dentist that grew up
thinking about kids first.

Sproutling started with one stubborn belief: a child's earliest experiences at the dentist shape how they care for their teeth for the rest of their life. So we made those experiences gentle, honest, and genuinely fun.

How we began

Founded by a pediatric dentist who was tired of tears.

Dr. Maya Okafor spent her early career in a large general practice, squeezing nervous toddlers into fifteen-minute slots between adult appointments. It never sat right. Kids were scared, parents were stressed, and "be brave" was doing a lot of heavy lifting.

In 2016 she opened a small clinic with a simple plan: design every detail — the light, the language, the pace — around how a young child actually feels. Word spread quickly among local parents, and Sproutling grew into a team of three specialists and a waiting room that sounds more like a playground than a clinic.

We're still small on purpose. It's the only way to give every family the unhurried, familiar care that made them choose us in the first place.

What we believe

Four things we never compromise on.

Calm over quick

A frightened child rushed through a checkup learns to fear the dentist. We'd rather take the extra ten minutes, every single time.

Honesty with parents

We explain every recommendation in plain language and never suggest treatment your child doesn't truly need. If watchful waiting is best, we'll say so.

Prevention beats repair

The best filling is the one we never have to do. We invest our time in coaching habits, sealing teeth, and catching small things early.

Every child belongs

Sensory needs, dental anxiety, special healthcare needs — our space and our team are built to welcome and adapt to every kid who walks in.

The space

Less waiting room, more wonder.

From the moment families walk in, the clinic is designed to feel safe, soft, and a little bit magical.

Beyond the chair

We're part of the neighbourhood.

Every spring we visit Maplewood's preschools and elementary schools with our "Happy Teeth Roadshow" — free brushing lessons, giant tooth puppets, and a toothbrush for every child. We also run a sliding-scale fund so cost is never the reason a kid misses care.
