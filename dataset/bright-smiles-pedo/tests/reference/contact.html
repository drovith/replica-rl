Finding us

Easy to reach, easy to park.

We're on the corner of Marigold Lane and Birch Street, two blocks from Maplewood Library. Look for the bright teal door and the giant smiling tooth in the window — you can't miss us.

Before you go

Worried it's an emergency?

If your child has knocked out a tooth, has a swollen face, or is in serious pain, call us straight away on (555) 014‑8800. We keep same-day slots open every day, and our after-hours line will guide you through what to do right now.
