01 · Trace explorer

From a vague "it's slow" to the exact span.

Compose queries the way you think about incidents — service, route, attribute, status, latency band — and get a ranked list of traces with full waterfalls behind each one.

  • →Attribute search across any tag you emit: tenant.id, db.statement, deploy.sha.
  • →Latency banding to isolate the p99 tail and compare it against the median request shape.
  • →Span diff — pin a healthy trace next to a slow one and highlight where they diverged.
explorer / querySpanQL
service = "checkout-svc"
  and route = "/v2/checkout"
  and duration > 250ms
  and span.db.system = "postgresql"
| group_by(span.db.statement)
| percentiles(duration, 50, 95, 99)
| sort(p99 desc)

03 · The rest of the toolkit

Everything else you reach for during an incident.

Span analytics

Aggregate any span attribute into time series on the fly. Build a p99-by-region chart from raw traces without pre-defining a single metric.

Trace-aware alerting

Alert on p99 of a route, error ratio of an edge, or saturation of a span attribute. Every alert links to exemplar traces that fired it.

Correlated logs

Logs carrying a trace ID snap straight into the waterfall. Read the exact log lines a slow span emitted without leaving the trace.

Deploy markers

Overlay every release on your latency charts. See the exact commit that bent your p99 and roll the comparison back to the second.

13-month hot retention

Compare this Black Friday's checkout path to last year's, span for span. Kept queryable, not cold-stored behind a restore job.

RBAC & audit

SSO via SAML or OIDC, scoped roles per team and per service, and a full audit log of every query and policy change.

04 · Cost controls

Observability that can't surprise your finance team.

Set ingest budgets per service. Spanforge enforces them with tail-sampling backpressure, so a runaway debug log or a retry storm tightens its own sample rate instead of detonating your invoice.

  • ✓Soft ceiling drops the baseline sample rate and posts to Slack.
  • ✓Hard ceiling caps ingest while still keeping 100% of errors.
  • ✓Marginal-value report ranks services by insight-per-dollar each week.
