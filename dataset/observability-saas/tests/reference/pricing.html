Why it's cheaper

Billing questions

No asterisks, no surprise overages.

Q1

What counts as a "retained span"?

Any span we store after your tail-sampling policy runs. Spans dropped by sampling are free — they never touch your meter. Errors are always retained and you're billed for them, because you'll always want them.

Q2

What happens if I hit my ingest budget?

Nothing breaks. Soft ceilings tighten your baseline sample rate and notify you; hard ceilings cap baseline ingest while still keeping 100% of errors. You decide the policy per service.

Q3

Is the free tier really free forever?

Yes. 50M retained spans a month, 30-day retention, no card required. It's a genuine production allowance for a small service, not a 14-day tease.

Q4

Do you charge for users or hosts?

No per-seat and no per-host fees on Team. Add your whole org. You're billed on retained spans and the flat platform fee — that's it.

Q5

Can we self-host?

On Enterprise, yes — fully in your own VPC or on-prem, with the same console and SDKs. Your trace data never leaves your network.
