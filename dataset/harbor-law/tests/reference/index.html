The Firm

A small bench of senior lawyers, built for the matters that cannot be delegated.

Halloran Voss was founded on a simple conviction: that the most consequential corporate decisions deserve the partners themselves, not a pyramid of associates.

We keep our docket deliberately narrow. Each client works directly with a named partner who stays on the matter from the first call through closing and beyond. That structure lets us move with the speed founders need and the rigor boards expect — whether we are negotiating a definitive agreement, defending a patent portfolio, or papering a term sheet at midnight before a board meeting.
