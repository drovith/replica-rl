Get started

Three steps to self-custody.

The whole flow is designed so a complete newcomer can finish it without help — and a veteran can move at full speed.

1

Download & create

Install the app or extension and tap "Create new wallet." Novault generates your recovery phrase on-device — no account, no email, no personal details.

2

Back up your phrase

Write down your 12 words and confirm them, or enable encrypted cloud backup. This is the master key to your funds — guard it and you're set.

3

Fund & explore

Receive crypto, buy with a card, or import an existing wallet. Novault auto-detects your assets across every chain and you're ready to go.

Switching over?

Bring your existing wallet with you.

Already on MetaMask, Phantom, Trust or a hardware wallet? Import with your recovery phrase or private key and Novault rebuilds your full multi-chain portfolio instantly — no funds move, nothing leaves your control.

Before you start

Quick answers for new holders.

Q. Do I need to give my email or ID?

No. Creating a Novault wallet requires no email, phone number or ID. KYC only ever applies if you choose to buy crypto with a card through a regulated provider.

Q. What's a recovery phrase?

It's 12 (or 24) words that are the master key to your wallet. Anyone with it controls your funds, so store it offline and never share it — Novault will never ask for it.

Q. Can I use it on phone and desktop?

Yes. Import the same recovery phrase on each device, or pair the extension with your phone. Your portfolio stays in sync because it all lives on-chain.

Q. Is there really nothing to pay?

Downloading, holding, sending and receiving are free. You only pay network gas and small, visible fees on optional swaps and on-ramps. See pricing.
