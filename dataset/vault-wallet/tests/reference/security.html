Security architecture

Not your keys?
Not your crypto.

Novault is built so that, by design, no employee, server or court order can move your assets. Here's exactly how the protection works — layer by layer, with nothing hidden behind marketing.

The trust model

Keys are born and die on your device.

When you create a wallet, your 12 or 24-word recovery phrase is generated locally using your device's hardware random number generator. From that phrase, every chain's private key is derived on-device. The phrase is encrypted at rest inside the Secure Enclave (iOS) or StrongBox / TEE (Android) and never transmitted — not to Novault, not to anyone.

  • No custody — we never hold, escrow or co-sign your funds
  • No accounts — no email or password to phish or leak
  • Keys never appear in plaintext outside secure hardware
  • Signing happens locally; we only broadcast signed transactions
Defence in depth

Six layers between you and a loss.

Self-custody removes the custodian as a single point of failure. We wrap the rest in overlapping protections so a single mistake is rarely fatal.

01

Hardware-backed key storage

Recovery phrases and private keys are sealed in the device Secure Enclave / TEE, gated by biometrics and never exportable in plaintext.

02

Transaction simulation

Every transaction is run against a fork of the live chain first, so you see token balance changes and approvals before you sign — not after.

03

Threat intelligence

Addresses, contracts and dapp domains are checked against a continuously updated registry of known drainers, phishing sites and malicious approvals.

04

Encrypted cloud recovery

Optional backup splits and encrypts your phrase before it ever touches iCloud or Google Drive. The decryption key never leaves your device.

05

Hardware wallet pairing

Connect a Ledger or Trezor to keep keys fully offline while still using Novault's interface, simulations and chain coverage.

06

Open-source & reproducible

Our signing and key-management code is public and builds are reproducible, so anyone can verify the binary matches the audited source.

Straight answers

Security questions, answered.

Q. What happens if I lose my phone?

Your funds live on the blockchain, not the phone. Reinstall Novault on any device, enter your recovery phrase (or restore the encrypted backup), and your full portfolio reappears.

Q. Can Novault freeze or seize my crypto?

No. We never hold your keys, so we have no technical ability to move, freeze or recover your assets — and neither does anyone who compels us.

Q. Is my recovery phrase ever sent anywhere?

Never in plaintext. If you enable cloud backup, the phrase is encrypted on-device first and the decryption key stays in your secure hardware.

Q. How are scams and drainers blocked?

We simulate transactions, label suspicious approvals, and screen dapp domains and addresses against a live threat registry, warning you before you sign.

Q. Do you support hardware wallets?

Yes. Pair a Ledger or Trezor to keep keys offline while using Novault's interface and chain coverage for everyday signing.

Q. Can I verify the code myself?

Yes. Our key-management and signing code is open source with reproducible builds, so you can confirm the app you run matches the audited source.
