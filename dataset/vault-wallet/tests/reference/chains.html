Everything in one place

A full crypto toolkit, not just a place to park tokens.

Best-price swaps

An on-board aggregator routes across DEXs and bridges to find the best execution, with slippage and the exact network fee shown before you confirm.

Cross-chain bridging

Move assets between Ethereum, Base, Solana and beyond without leaving the app. Routes are simulated and quoted so there are no surprises mid-bridge.

Native staking

Stake ETH, SOL, MATIC and more in a couple of taps, track your rewards over time, and unstake whenever you like — non-custodial throughout.

NFT & collectibles gallery

Your ERC-721s, Solana NFTs and Bitcoin Ordinals in one tidy gallery, with spam filtering and rich previews. Send and list without a separate app.

WalletConnect & dapp browser

Connect to thousands of dapps on mobile via WalletConnect, or browse the open economy directly in the extension. Sessions are visible and revocable.

ENS & human names

Send to vitalik.eth instead of a 42-character string. Novault resolves ENS, SNS and Unstoppable names and warns on look-alikes.

Portfolio & history

A unified view of every asset across every chain, with cost-basis, performance over time, and exportable transaction history for tax season.

Multiple wallets & vaults

Spin up separate wallets for trading, savings or a DAO, each with its own name and colour, all managed from one secure app.

On & off ramps

Buy with a card. Cash out to your bank.

Novault partners with regulated providers so you can move between fiat and crypto without leaving self-custody. Funds land directly in your wallet — never an exchange balance you have to withdraw.

  • Buy with card, Apple Pay or bank transfer in 30+ currencies
  • Sell to fiat and withdraw straight to your bank account
  • Provider quotes compared side by side for the best rate
  • Assets arrive in your wallet — you stay in control
