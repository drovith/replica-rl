Slow Return

Our cornerstone week and the truest expression of what Fallow is. Two daily practices, restorative evenings, long open afternoons, and one full day of held silence in the middle — the day most people remember years later. There is no agenda but the gradual unclenching of a nervous system that forgot it was allowed to.

The Long Exhale

Our most spacious program, for people coming out of a hard season — grief, burnout, a long stretch of caring for others. The same, on every program

What your rate always covers.

Every practice

Two daily yoga and breath sessions in the barn, plus any optional sittings and walks. All levels are held in the same gentle room — beginners are genuinely welcome.

All meals

Three vegetable-forward meals a day from the kitchen garden and nearby farms, plus tea, fruit and good bread always out in the long room. Dietary needs met without fuss.

A simple room

Your own quiet room with wool blankets, fresh linens, towels and a window onto the fields. A handful of shared rooms are offered at a lower rate for those who prefer them.

The whole land

Free run of twenty acres — the meadows, the woodland, the pond, the orchard and six miles of walkable lanes from the gate before you meet a road.

Held silence

Facilitated periods of quiet, from a single silent morning on the weekend to two full days on the longer programs. Always optional, always supported.

Time with teachers

You'll practise with the same small faculty all stay. On the longer programs you can book one-to-one restorative or breath sessions at no extra cost.

Holding a room is simple
