Before you go

Read the small print

Times can shift

Live festivals breathe. Set times may move by up to 30 minutes on the day — check the boards at each stage and the RIPTIDE app on site.

Plan one clash

You cannot see everything, and trying to ruins the weekend. Pick one must-see per slot, then let the rest surprise you.

Mind the tide

High Tide and the beach bars sit below the tide line. At peak high water the beachfront path closes for 40 minutes — use the dune walkway instead.
