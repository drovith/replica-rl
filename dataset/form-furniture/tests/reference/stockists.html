Before you ask

Common questions

Can I buy directly?

Yes. Private buyers can order through any stockist or straight from the studio by email. We'll confirm finishes, take a deposit, and book the workshop slot.

What are the lead times?

Most pieces ship 8–10 weeks from order. Travertine tops take 12–14 weeks because each is cut and honed to match. We'll always give you a firm date in writing.

Do you ship internationally?

We ship worldwide, crated and insured. Larger pieces go by white-glove delivery; we'll quote freight with your order and handle customs paperwork.

Can I get samples?

Yes — request a swatch box with any enquiry. It holds the oak and walnut finishes, leather and wool, and a travertine chip, dispatched within five days.

What if something needs repair?

Send us a photo. We keep every part in stock for the life of a design, refill cushions for free, and share drawings so a local maker can help if you're far away.
