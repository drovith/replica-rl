The design

One frame, one sling, one cushion

Fold 01 began as a sketch of the fewest parts a comfortable chair could have. The frame is four oak members joined with through-tenons you can see from the side. A single piece of vegetable-tanned leather is stretched between them to form both seat and back — it gives a little, then settles to your shape.

The cushion is the only soft thing: feather-and-down wrapped around a foam core, loose so it can be turned, plumped, or sent back to us for a refill years from now.

Nothing is hidden. The hardware is honest steel, the oil is something you can re-apply with a cloth, and every joint can be tightened with a single hex key kept under the seat.

Living with it

Care, in three lines

Wipe the oak with a damp cloth; re-oil once a year or whenever it looks thirsty. Let the leather take on the marks of being used — they're the point.

Turn the cushion now and then. When it tires, we'll re-fill it rather than replace it.
