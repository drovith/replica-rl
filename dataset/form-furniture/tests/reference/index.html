The idea

We design slowly and edit hard. Every piece earns its place by doing one thing exactly right — and nothing it doesn't need to. What remains is quiet, useful, and built to be lived with for decades.

Designed to last

A chair you repair, not replace

The Quoin dining chair is jointed, not glued in haste. Its seat unscrews; its leather can be re-stretched; its frame can be re-oiled at home with a cloth. Nothing is sealed shut against the people who own it.

We keep every component in stock for the life of the piece — so a worn cushion or a loosened joint is a half-hour fix, never a reason to start again.

Materials & craft

Five materials, deeply understood

Oiled European oak. Blackened mild steel. Full-grain vegetable-tanned leather. Undyed Italian wool. Honed travertine. We work with a short list on purpose — the more we narrow the palette, the better we know it.
